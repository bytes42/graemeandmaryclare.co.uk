How do I get there?

Church

There is parking for those wishing to drive to the church.

The best public transport option is the overland to Edmonton Green then a 5 minute walk.

If you'd prefer the Underground then head to Tottenham Hale or Seven Sisters and either catch the overland or a bus to Edmonton Green. From here it's a 5 minute walk.

Reception

A coach will be taking guests from the church to the reception

Those coming for the evening we recommend get a train from King's Cross to Hatfield Station and then either catch a short taxi ride or 2 buses.

There's parking at the reception for those taking a car - here is a suggested route.

Home

If you don't fancy heading back to London they'll be a minibus running from the reception to the Premier Inn, Hatfield. From there you can get a train to King's Cross the following day.

We're more than happy to help organise travel - London is a big place! The TFL website also has a very good journey planner.

Is there a hotel nearby?

Rooms are available at Tewin Bury Farm for between £119-£139, including breakfast. Rooms are limited so if you'd like one please let us know as soon as possible.

There is also the Premier Inn, Hatfield which has rooms available from £75. We'll be running a minibus from the reception to here.
